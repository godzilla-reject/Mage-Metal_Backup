# Skill Description-`=this.file.name`
**`=this.file.name`** is the skill of taunting an enemy into attacking *you*, instead of your allies. It is *not* the skill of making enemies fearful of you; that would be [[Intimidation]]. **`=this.file.name`** is often used by those who wish to protect allies and draw enemy fire.

# Skill Actions
#### Untrained Actions
These actions can be performed without any training in `=this.file.name `; they are available to any character. 
> [!note]+ ##### Draw Ire ●
> [[Mental]]
>- - -
> You exert your physical prowess, drawing the aggression of an enemy. Choose an enemy that you are aware of that can perceive you. Make a **`=this.file.name`** contest against its **Mind**. On a success, it becomes [[Taunted\|Taunted 1]]. This action gains either the [[Auditory]] and [[Vocal]] traits if you vocalize in order to Draw Ire, or [[Somatic]] if you make a gesture.

#### Trained Actions
These actions can be performed by characters who are *Trained* in `=this.file.name`; they are not available to characters with lesser training.
> [!note]+ ##### Ire Stance ●●
> [[Auditory]], [[Mental]], [[Vocal]], [[Stance]]
>- - -
> When you enter this stance, make a **`=this.file.name`** contested by **Mind** for all enemies within a 4-space burst, centered on you. On a success, that enemy becomes [[Taunted\|Taunted 1]]. At the start of each of your turns that you are in this stance, you can Draw Ire as a free action.

# Perks
>> [!info|text-Center clear]+ Trained Perk: Defensive Taunt
>> *When you become Trained in `=this.file.name`, if you have aptitude in it, you gain the following benefits.*
>> You gain an untyped bonus to Toughness against attacks from enemies that are [[Taunted]] by you. This bonus is equal to the enemy’s Taunted value. 

>> [!info|text-Center clear]+ Expertise Perk: Scornful Aura
>> *When you become an Expert in `=this.file.name`, if you have aptitude in it, you gain the following benefits.*
>> Whenever you gain this perk, you gain a 2-space aura of scorn. Any enemy within this aura makes contests against your **`=this.file.name`** checks with a -3 difficulty penalty. When you enter Ire Stance, Scornful Aura grows to 4 spaces.

>> [!info|text-Center clear]+ Masterful Perk: Keep Sway
>> *When you become a Master in `=this.file.name`, if you have aptitude in it, you gain the following benefits.*
>> If a creature taunted by you would leave your Scornful Aura, you can Move up to your Speed as a free action, but you must use this movement to follow that enemy. 

>> [!info|text-Center clear]+ Legendary Perk: Perfect Prowess
>> *When you become a Legend in `=this.file.name`, if you have aptitude in it, you gain the following benefits.*
>> Whenever you Draw Ire while in Ire Stance, you do so against *all enemies within your Scornful Aura.* Additionally, increase the value of all [[Taunted]] conditions you inflict by 2. Finally, any creature that is [[Taunted\|Taunted 4]] or more by you is also [[Off-Guard]] to you and your allies.
